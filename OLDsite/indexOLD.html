About Me

Hi and welcome to my personal website. My name is Micah and I am an inspired future Full Stack web developer that is from Oklahoma City. I first started out doing web design in the spring of 2015 taking online classes at Code School and Lynda. This experience soon landed me my first full time job as a web designer at the current company I am at. Unfortunately it did not last that long because of cutbacks the company had to make, so I was placed back at my senior tech roll as I was before but still help the design team as an additional role.
